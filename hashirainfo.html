A Guide to Demon Slaying

「 KNYDEX 」


「 The Stone Pillar 」

Gyomei Himejima is the current Stone Pillar of the Demon Slayer Corps. He is a tall, muscular man with a rather intimidating aura, however, he is a kind and gentle soul at heart. He is quite disciplined and religious, often seen holding his prayer beads. Gyomei is blind, but he is not to be underestimated during battle. The other pillars and members of the Corps acknowledge him to be an incredibly strong warrior–one of, if not the strongest of his generation. He wields a unique weapon, a flail and axe, which he uses alongside Stone Breathing.


「 The Wind Pillar 」

Sanemi Shinazugawa is the current Wind Pillar of the Demon Slayer Corps. He is known for his aggressive and volatile nature, being hot-tempered, blunt, and often cruel with his words, especially toward those he believes are weak. He has spiky white hair, sharp eyes, and a body covered in scars from countless battles with demons. Despite this harsh exterior, he deeply cares for others. He harbors an intense hatred for demons due to the trauma of his past. Sanemi’s fighting style is wild and relentless, matching his Wind Breathing techniques. Beneath his rage, he is a deeply wounded and loyal man who bears his pain in silence.


「 The Water Pillar 」

Giyu Tomioka is the current Water Pillar of the Demon Slayer Corps and one of its most skilled swordsmen. He has long, dark hair, sharp blue eyes, and wears a half-and-half haori that once belonged to his late friends. Giyu is quiet, stoic, and socially awkward, often coming across as cold or distant to others. In reality, he is deeply kind and compassionate, though he struggles with guilt and feelings of unworthiness. Giyu, despite his self-doubts, is much more skilled than he thinks–having mastered Water Breathing and even creating his own technique. He was the one who spared Tanjiro and Nezuko, and alongside his master, Urokodaki–vowed to commit seppuku if Nezuko ever ate a human.


「 The Flame Pillar 」

Kyojuro Rengoku is the current Flame Pillar of the Demon Slayer Corps and is known for his overwhelming enthusiasm and strength. He has bright yellow hair with red tips, thick eyebrows, and fiery golden eyes that reflect his passionate spirit. Rengoku is loud, cheerful, and confident, often speaking with dramatic intensity. He strongly believes in honoring his duty and protecting the weak, a philosophy passed down by his mother. As a swordsman, he wields Flame Breathing with powerful, explosive techniques. Rengoku is deeply caring and encouraging, especially towards younger demon slayers–his unwavering resolve and noble heart leaving a lasting impact on everyone he meets.


「 The Love Pillar 」

Mitsuri Kanroji is the current Love Pillar of the Demon Slayer Corps and is known for her warmth and emotional openness. She has long pink hair with green tips, large green eyes, and a gentle, soft appearance. She originally had black hair as a child, however, her hair turned pink and green after eating large amounts of sakura mochi. Mitsuri is extremely kind, affectionate, and expressive, often wearing her heart on her sleeve. Despite her sweetness, she possesses incredible physical strength and flexibility. She joined the Corps to find a husband stronger than her, then became Rengoku’s student. She uses Love Breathing, a unique style derived from her teacher’s Flame Breathing style, and wields a unique, thin, whip-like sword. Mitsuri deeply values her comrades and fights with intense love and devotion. Her compassionate nature and bravery make her one of the most heartfelt members of the Hashira.


「 The Serpent Pillar 」

Obanai Iguro is the current Serpent Pillar of the Demon Slayer Corps and is known for his strict and severe demeanor. He has straight black hair, heterochromic eyes, and his lower face is wrapped in bandages due to an incident from his past–leaving scars across his mouth. Obanai is harsh, sharp-tongued, and deeply intolerant of those who break the rules. He is extremely loyal to the Corps’ leader, and holds himself to rigid standards. Obanai uses Serpent Breathing, a unique style that uses a unique, twisted sword. He is partially blind in his right eye, thus having a snake named Kaburamaru as his animal guide to aid him in battle.


「 The Mist Pillar 」

Muichiro Tokito is the current Mist Pillar of the Demon Slayer Corps and the youngest member of the Hashira, currently fourteen, and becoming a Pillar two months after picking up a sword for the first time. He has long black hair that fades into teal, along with distant, unfocused eyes that often seem lost in thought. Muichiro is usually apathetic, blank, and forgetful, appearing detached from his surroundings. This behavior is due to his amnesia and emotional trauma from his past after losing his twin brother. In battle, he becomes sharp, ruthless, and incredibly skilled. Despite his aloof nature, Muichiro possesses a kind heart and strong sense of justice. At his core, Muichiro is a childish and cheerful soul.

「 The Sound Pillar 」

Tengen Uzui is the current Sound Pillar of the Demon Slayer Corps and values flamboyance above all else. He has a tall, muscular build, white hair, painted red markings around his eye, and overall a very flashy appearance. Tengen is loud, confident, and dramatic, often boasting about his strength. Despite this, he is surprisingly caring and respectful toward others, especially towards his three wives. He wields dual cleavers connected by a chain and uses Sound Breathing. He comes from a family of ninjas, and applies his techniques to demon slaying. Tengen is extremely tactical, analyzing enemy movements. Beneath his flamboyant nature, he has a strong sense of responsibility and honor.


「 The Insect Pillar 」

Shinobu Kocho is the current Insect Pillar of the Demon Slayer Corps. She has gentle purple eyes, a constant sweet smile, and dark hair tied back with a butterfly-shaped clip. Despite her calm and cheerful demeanor, she carries sharp intelligence and suppressed anger within her. Shinobu lacks the physical strength to decapitate demons, so she relies on poison derived from wisteria flowers. Her fighting style is elegant and swift, resembling the delicate movements of an insect in flight. As a skilled doctor, she is responsible for treating injured Demon Slayers and researching demon biology. She often teases others with a soft voice and polite words that contrast with her sarcasm. She holds a deep grief over her sister’s death at the hands of an Upper Moon Demon, and this pain fuels her determination to eradicate demons while protecting human life. Shinobu embodies the contrast between beauty and danger, kindness and vengeance.


「 Tanjiro Kamado 」

Tanjiro Kamado is a skilled swordsman in the Demon Slayer Corps. He has warm reddish-brown eyes, a distinct scar on his forehead, and bright eyes that reflect his warm personality. Tanjiro possesses an extraordinary sense of smell, which helps him track demons and read emotions in battle. Despite enduring immense hardship, he remains compassionate, even toward his enemies. He trains relentlessly to grow stronger so he can protect others and turn his sister, Nezuko, into human once more. Tanjiro practices Water Breathing and later incorporates elements of Hinokami Kagura into his fighting style. His politeness and empathy often inspire those around him to persevere. He carries deep guilt and responsibility for his family’s fate, which shapes his resolve. Tanjiro’s moral strength is as powerful as his physical abilities.


「 Zenitsu Agatsuma 」

Zenitsu Agatsuma is a loud, anxious, yet surprisingly powerful swordsman in the Demon Slayer Corps. He has short, choppy blond hair and often wears a yellow haori patterned with small white triangles. Zenitsu is extremely fearful of demons and frequently panics or cries in dangerous situations. Despite this, he possesses exceptional hearing that allows him to detect even the subtlest sounds and movements. When overwhelmed by fear, Zenitsu often falls unconscious, revealing his true combat potential. In this state, he becomes calm, focused, and incredibly fast. He practices Thunder Breathing, which he learned from the previous Thunder Pillar. Zenitsu is deeply loyal to his friends, and especially protective of Nezuko. Beneath fear and cowardice, lies a sincere heart that longs to be brave.

「 Inosuke Hashibara 」

Inosuke Hashibira is a wild and aggressive swordsman of the Demon Slayer Corps. He is easily recognized by the boar’s head mask he wears, which hides his surprisingly beautiful face. Inosuke was raised in the mountains, giving him a feral personality and incredible physical strength. He constantly seeks fights and views almost everyone as a rival. Despite his rough behavior, he gradually learns the value of teamwork and friendship. Inosuke created his own unique Beast Breathing style, which is unpredictable and brutal. He wields dual swords, which he breaks to make it more barbed–much to his swordsmiths’ dismay. He has an unusual ability to dislocate and rearrange his joints to evade attacks, and a heightened sense of touch that allows him to feel vibrations in the air and sense enemies.
